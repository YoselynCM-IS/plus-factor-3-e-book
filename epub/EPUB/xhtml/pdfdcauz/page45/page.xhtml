POSSIBILITY

 

We use  
may /might / could 
to express possibility in the present  

 

or future.

 

Where’s Rita? She  
may
be at work.

 

We use  
might 
to express small possibility or unreal situations.

 

If I had money, I  
might 
buy that car.

 

May not 
and  
Might 
not express lack of possibility in the present.

 

Sally  
may not / might not 
be at home now.

 

Note: 
May
is more formal.
