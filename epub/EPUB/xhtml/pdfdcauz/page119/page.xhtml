Learning Evidence

 

Write an INFORMAL and a FORMAL letter with the following information.

 

You are studying English at a private language school. You are very happy because you

 

have met new friends from foreign countries. However, one of your classmates has been

 

feeling very sad because he/she misses his/her family. You are planning a surprise party to

 

cheer him/her up and let him/her know all of your classmates are there to back him/her up.

 

I.
Write an INFORMAL letter to a friend (classmate) and invite him/her to the party.

 

In your letter:

 

a.
explain the reason for the party

 

b.
give the date and time of the party

 

c.
suggest what the classmate could bring
