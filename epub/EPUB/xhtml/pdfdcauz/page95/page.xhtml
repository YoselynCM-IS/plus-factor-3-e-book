GRAMMAR 
:
Present perfect

 

Is used:

 

For an action which happened in the past but we don’t know when exactly:

 

o

 

I have been in India.

 

For an action which happened in the past but its results are obvious in the present:

 

o

 

Sandy has broken her arm.

 

For an action which started in the past and continues up to the present:

 

o

 

They have practiced karate for ten years
