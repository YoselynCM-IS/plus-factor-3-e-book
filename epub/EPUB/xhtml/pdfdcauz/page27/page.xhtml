Wacky predictions for the future

 

1.
Your kitchen will refill itself. The kitchen of tomorrow

 

will have containers which will send out alerts, when

2.
You’ll check email with contact lenses.

 

 
Engineers at Samsung 
®

3.
We’ll have dinosaur zoos with real woolly mammoths.

 

 
The advances in cloning technology, will be able to

 

bring back extinct animals. Akira Iritani, a professor at

 

Kyoto University, declares “the technical problems have

 

been overcome, all we need is a good sample of soft

 

tissue from a frozen mammoth.”

 

4.
CGI (computer-generated imagery) will replace actors entirely.

 

CGI has been used for everything from creating new scenes

 

of actors in their youth to replacing actors who’ve died.

 

5.
You’ll fly in planes that are literally all window.

 

 
If the Center for Process Innovation have its way,

 

everybody will get a window seat in planes which

 

will offer panoramic views of the sky. The windows

 

aren’t technically real, they’re just cameras

 

mounted on the plane’s exterior. Happy flying!
