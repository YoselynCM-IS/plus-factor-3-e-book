In this unit you will:

will / going to 
)

 

Learning outcome

 

•
Students make an argument about the main reason to visit some places around the world

 

and to know about the culture of the place.

 

Learning evidence

 

•
Students elaborate a collage with graphic content and expose the main reasons they have

 

to visit their places of interest.
