Do you know what dysfunctional thoughts are?

 

mind

 

They are thoughts that cause us worry, anguish, fear or anger by

 

distracting us and negatively influencing our decisions. For example:

 

1.
I arrived at the classroom and nobody greeted me. Nobody

 

loves me.

 

2.
I got a red spot on my arm, for sure it's skin cancer.

 

3.
If Luis, who is a genius, got 8 in the exam, I surely got a 5!

 

4.
I fell in front of the cafeteria and everyone laughed at me; I'm a

 

failure, everything goes wrong.

 

5.
The teacher graded me a 7 in the project because I do not

 

like him.
